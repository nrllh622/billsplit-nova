Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BillSplit Nova ("the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

No Personal Data Collection

BillSplit Nova is designed with your privacy in mind. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not require account creation, sign-up, or login.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers that identify you personally.

Local Data Storage

Any data you enter into the App — such as bill amounts, tip percentages, currency preferences, and saved calculation history — is stored locally on your device only. This data is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Currency Exchange Rate Data

To provide live currency conversion, the App connects to a third-party exchange rate API service over a secure (HTTPS) connection. This request only includes the currency codes needed to fetch exchange rates and does not include any personal information about you.

Children's Privacy

BillSplit Nova does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App is a general-purpose calculation tool suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
